my games

these games were made with kaboomjs while i was introducing my nieces/nephews to coding.

  • watermelon rush - eat as many watermelons as you can before time runs out
  • watermelon hunt - find the watermelon without hitting any obstacles
  • air hockey - player one uses a+d, player two uses left+right. Touch controls on touch devices
  • flappy hurdler - jump over the obstacles
  • flappy dodge - dodge the watermelons
  • flappy watermelon feast - catch as many watermelons as you can
